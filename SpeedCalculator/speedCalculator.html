About Speed Units

Metric System

The SI unit for speed is meters per second (m/s). Common alternatives include kilometers per hour (km/h) for road speeds and millimeters per second (mm/s) for precision measurements.

Imperial/US Units

Miles per hour (mph) is standard for road speeds in the US and UK. Feet per second (ft/s) is common in engineering, while knots are used in marine navigation.

Aviation & Space

Mach number represents speed relative to the speed of sound (Mach 1 ≈ 343 m/s at sea level). The speed of light (c) is approximately 299,792,458 m/s in vacuum.

Conversions

1 mph = 1.60934 km/h = 0.44704 m/s = 0.868976 knots. Mach 1 varies with altitude and temperature but is roughly 767.269 mph at sea level.
