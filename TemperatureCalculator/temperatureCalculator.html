Temperature Conversion Formulas

Celsius (°C)

°F = (°C × 9/5) + 32
K = °C + 273.15
°R = (°C + 273.15) × 9/5

Fahrenheit (°F)

°C = (°F - 32) × 5/9
K = (°F - 32) × 5/9 + 273.15
°R = °F + 459.67

Kelvin (K)

°C = K - 273.15
°F = (K × 9/5) - 459.67
°R = K × 9/5

Rankine (°R)

°C = (°R - 491.67) × 5/9
°F = °R - 459.67
K = °R × 5/9
