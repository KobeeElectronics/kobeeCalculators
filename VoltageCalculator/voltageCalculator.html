Peak Voltage (Vp)

Vp = Vp-p ÷ 2

Vp = Vrms × √2

Vp = π × Vavg ÷ 2

Peak-to-Peak Voltage (Vp-p)

Vp-p = 2 × Vp

Vp-p = 2√2 × Vrms

Vp-p = π × Vavg

RMS Voltage (Vrms)

Vrms = Vp ÷ √2

Vrms = Vp-p ÷ 2√2

Vrms = π × Vavg ÷ 2√2

Average Voltage (Vavg)

Vavg = 2 × Vp ÷ π

Vavg = Vp-p ÷ π

Vavg = 2√2 × Vrms ÷ π
